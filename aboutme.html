About Me



Currently, I am a graudate of the Rochester Institute of Technology with a degree in Advertising and Public Relations. This may seem like an oxymoron, but RIT is more than just a tech school even though I've learned a lot more tech things in these years than I would have thought.

I am a 23 year old Dominican native who moved to New Bedford, MA many years ago with my family in an effort to realize all of our dreams. In May, I will be the first person in my family to graduate from college which is something I am very proud of.

My dream is to one day be an event planner extradionare for non-profits, with a side job in advertising and enough money to watch a musical or concert every week. If this does not work out, I know how to write, research, be creative and cook.
